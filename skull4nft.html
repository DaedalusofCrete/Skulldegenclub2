SKULL 4 NFT

SKULL4NFT is a grass-roots initiative by artists within the SKULL community to collab with the SKULL Degen Club by primary collectors receiving a designated amount of $SKULL, and the final piece by the collab artist to ONLY sell for $SKULL token.
